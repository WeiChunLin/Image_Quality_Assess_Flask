Image Qaulity Assessment Tool


The Image Quality Assessment tool is a software program that allows users to upload images and assess their quality based on the image quality index. The tool is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, with a simple and intuitive user interface that allows users to quickly upload their images and obtain quality scores.

The tool uses the OpenCV library to read the images, and calculates the quality index by analyzing the intensity ratio and texture smoothness ratio of the images. The intensity ratio is calculated by analyzing the contrast between the lightest and darkest pixels in the image, while the texture smoothness ratio is calculated by analyzing the amount of noise in the image.
